About ENC

Since its founding in 2012, ENC Co., Ltd. has continuously pushed the boundaries of research and development to deliver exceptional products. Now, moving beyond its roots as a sheet mask specialist, ENC is taking on an even bolder challenge: becoming a total cosmetics company that brings the excellence of K-beauty to the world.

More

  • OEM

    A customizing system that proposes and supplies products tailored to the client's exact specifications, from formulation through to manufacturing and production

  • ODM

    A consulting system that proposes and supplies finished products, covering everything from formulation and packaging components to design

Sheet Mask

Facial masks crafted from a diverse range of nonwoven fabrics and packaging formats

Single Use

Single-use products featuring a variety of packaging formats, including pouches, stick pouches, and blister packs

Skin Care

A broad range of skincare and functional cosmetics designed to help maintain a healthy skin environment

Hydrogel

Hydrogel products applicable across a wide range of formats, from facial masks and spot patches to special care treatments
