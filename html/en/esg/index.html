Environment

Based on our in-house R&D capabilities, we create environmentally friendly cosmetics.

ENC prioritizes the use of environmentally safe ingredients from the product development stage and manages manufacturing processes to minimize energy waste.
We systematically reduce waste and energy consumption generated during production, while focusing on developing sustainable formulations using naturally derived ingredients.
Through differentiated research technologies, we strive to deliver sustainable beauty solutions that are safe for the skin and gentle on the environment.

Social

Governance
